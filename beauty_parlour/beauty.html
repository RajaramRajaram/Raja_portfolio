01 — Overview

About the project

"Customer Management & Invoice System"

Many small beauty parlours still rely on notebooks or spreadsheets to manage customer information and billing. This often leads to misplaced records, billing errors, and difficulty tracking returning customers.

The Beauty Parlour App was developed to solve these problems by providing a centralized digital platform where salon owners can securely store customer details, generate invoices, and access previous visit history from their mobile devices.

The application is designed with simplicity in mind, allowing even non-technical users to manage their business efficiently.

I owned the project end-to-end: designing the UX flow, building the React Native client, and wiring up the backend that powers bookings, notifications, and payments.

06 — Problems Solved

Challenges

Designing an Easy-to-Use Interface

The application needed to be simple enough for beauty parlour staff with limited technical knowledge.

Managing Real-Time Data

Firebase Firestore was integrated to synchronize customer information across sessions.

Data Validation

Implemented validation to prevent incomplete customer records and invalid invoice entries.

Performance Optimization

Optimized Firestore queries to ensure fast loading even with a large customer database.
